Our Story

Founded by Bev Shaw (now House), our firm began as part of Brunsdon Financial and is now independently owned and operated by Bev. From the outset, the business has been built on a commitment to outstanding customer service, professional integrity, and long-term relationships.

Bev's hands-on approach and passion for delivering high-quality advice have resulted in an exceptionally loyal client base, ranging from first-time buyers to experienced professionals with more complex needs. We take pride in offering clear, practical guidance, ensuring our clients feel informed, supported, and confident in their decisions.

We work with a wide range of lenders and providers, as well as several large employers who trust us to support their employees with mortgage and protection advice. Our services are designed to be accessible and personal, while maintaining the highest standards of compliance and professionalism.

As a proud partner of The Openwork Partnership, we operate within a strong regulatory framework that supports robust governance, quality assurance, and access to the wider market — allowing us to deliver sound financial advice to clients across the UK.
